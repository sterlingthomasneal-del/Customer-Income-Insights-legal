2. Service Description

Customer Income Score is a Shopify application that provides address-based wealth segmentation for marketing purposes. The App:

  • Reads each customer's shipping address from your Shopify store.
  • Estimates the property value or typical rental cost associated with that address using Google LLC's Gemini service as a sub-processor (see Privacy Policy and DPA for details).
  • Applies a deterministic algorithm to the estimated property value, geographic cost-of-living multiplier, and housing type to assign each customer a wealth score on a 1–10 scale and a segment label (Budget, Middle, Upper-Middle, or Affluent). The scoring formula is fixed; the same inputs always produce the same output.
  • Tags customers in your Shopify store with their segment and score for use in your marketing tools.
  • Provides a dashboard with aggregate statistics, segment distributions, and marketing guidance.

All estimates are approximations derived from publicly available geographic and property data. They are directional marketing insights only — not verified facts about any individual, and not suitable for any decision that produces legal or similarly significant effects on a person.

2.4 How the Score Is Computed

The wealth score is computed by a deterministic algorithm, not by AI. Google LLC's Gemini service is used as a sub-processor to provide one narrow input: an estimated property value (or typical rental cost) for the shipping address. Our published scoring algorithm then combines that estimate with a cost-of-living multiplier for the state and a housing-type factor to produce a fixed, reproducible score. The same address always produces the same score.

This matters because it means the App does not make automated decisions about individuals using generative or machine-learning classification. The only step that is model-assisted is property-value estimation, which is equivalent in character to looking up an address on Zillow or Redfin and is itself derived from public property records.

  • Customer email addresses — Read transiently from Shopify to locate a customer record, but never written to our database.

  • Payment card numbers or financial account information
  • Social Security numbers or government-issued IDs
  • Order history, purchase amounts, or transaction data
  • Browsing behavior, cookies, or tracking pixels
  • Actual income, tax returns, or credit reports
  • Any data that crosses shop boundaries — all stored data is partitioned by shop domain
